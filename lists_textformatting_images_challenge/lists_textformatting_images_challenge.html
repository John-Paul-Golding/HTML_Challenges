5-minute Cheesecake Everyone is talking about

Ingredients
  • 1L tub of yoghurt (I recommend granadilla flavour)
  • 1 tin of condensed milk
  • 1 packet of tennis biscuits
  • About two tablespoons of melted butter to bind the base together.

Directions

  1. Crush the tennis biscuits for the base (use a rolling pin to crush it quicker).
  2. Melt the butter and pour it over the biscuit.
  3. Mix well until it starts binding together. Spread until it is evened out
  4. Mix the yoghurt and condensed milk together in a bowl.
  5. Heat in the microwave for 2 minutes.
  6. Take the mixture out, mix quickly and put back in the microwave for another 2 minutes.
  7. Pour the mixure over the tennis biscuit base.
  8. Put the cheesecake in the fridge to cool down.
  9. Enjoy
